\subsection*{Solution}

Lets recall from \cite{kamen2000fundamentals} that:
\begin{equation*}
x(t) = a_0 + \displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} A_k \cos(k \omega_0 t + \theta_k) \;
-\infty < t < \infty 
\end{equation*} 

\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
X_0 = a_0 \\
|X_n| &= \frac{1}{2} A_k \; , k=1,2,.. \\
\angle X_n &= \theta_k \; , k=1,2,..
\label{eq:c1p31}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}

\item
